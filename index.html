About Us

At A. W. Carter, craftsmanship and quality underpin every aspect of our business. From fine furniture for elegant settings, to modern, minimalist cabinetry, we are committed to beautiful design and durable construction. If you want the highest quality available in your new kitchen, or are looking to replicate an antique armoire, we can do it all. With over 100 years of combined experience in the design and construction of exceptional furniture and cabinetry, you can count on us. If it’s not perfect, it’s not right.
